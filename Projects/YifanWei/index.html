Top 14 Deadliest Terrorist Groups

2000 - 2015

Total Incident: 87,009; Total Deaths: 207,830

During 2000 to 2015, more than two hundreds thousands of lives perished in terrorist attack.

The top 3 deadliest terrorist groups in recent time are Islamic State of Iraq, Taliban and Boko Haram. 9 of 14 top terrorist groups are Islamic-related.

The top 5 countries that suffered most are Iraq, Afghanistan, Nigeria, Syria and Yemen.

Color Scheme: 9 Islamic-related terrorist groups have verying shade of red color gradient to yellow color. The other 5 terrorist groups have varying shades of red color gradient to purple color.

Color Scheme: Middle-Eastern countries have purple shades; South Asian countries have green shades; African countries have blue shades; Other countries are marked with different colors.
