Population in 2100! What the millenials need to know

By : Bharath Nagesh

Africa will be the center of population growth in the coming century.

Countries like Nigeria, Tanzania and Ethiopia will witness an exponential increase in population.

India's and China's population will stabilize around 2020 and 2060 respectively and then decline by the end of the century.
